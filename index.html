Hi, I'm Sian Phillips. With a background in statistics, coding, and design, I have a natural affinity for data visualisation. This practice allows me to combine my passion for numbers and problem-solving with my creative instincts, turning complex data problems into visually compelling graphics.

I graduated with a degree in Mathematics and Statistics from Edinburgh University and more recently completed a master's degree in Data Visualisation at Warwick University.

This portfolio encapsulates some of my recent work related to data and design.
