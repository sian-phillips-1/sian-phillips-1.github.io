# Chilcare Costs 

The target audience are:
- central government and local authority stakeholders
- knowledgeable but non-technicalpeople with varying ability to read and understand data;
- local/regional policymakers
- local politicians
- national policymakers
- national politicians
- partner and anchor organisations
- other organisations across the public, private,voluntary sectors that play a role in shaping our local and regional economy
- members of the public - generally non-technical non-scientific members of the public

The key message is that childcare costs are disproportionately 
borne by our most deprivedhouseholds, and has a gendered dimension to this. Infographics and visualisations should makevisible the challenges here.
