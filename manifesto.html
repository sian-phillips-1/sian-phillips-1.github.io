Data Design Manifesto

This is my design manifesto that states my approach to data visualisation and my relationship with concepts and values that are important to my practice.

The Process

I created collages using magazine and newspaper cut-outs that aligned with the themes and inspirations of my manifesto. This hand-crafted element adds a deeper layer of personalisation, reflecting the overall intention of the project. Just like a collage, my manifesto is a patchwork of styles, drawing insights from the data professionals who have influenced me, and arranging them into my own unique vision.

The idea of liberation emerged when reflecting on the purpose of data design and the impact I would like my work to have. This direction brings everything together, connecting my past inspirations with the vision guiding my future aspirations.

The collage pages were scanned into In-Design where the text was added in, along with the other finishing touches.
